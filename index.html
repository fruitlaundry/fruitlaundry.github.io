about

Fruit Laundry creates experimental theatrical performances. These performances arise from its two primary projects:

  • the research and development of a system of making performance that centres the enactment, accumulation, repetition, and negotiation of shared agreements; and

  • the research and development of performance between theatre and new media, striving for the point where the veil is the thinnest; the membrane the most porous; the distinction the most unstable
upcoming
